About Us

MZ Cars – selling quality cars from Europe. Our experience and expertise allow us to select worthy vehicles from all over Europe and bring them to Latvia for further sale.

In our fleet, you can always find interesting cars for almost every taste. If something is not available, we can always bring it on order. You can buy a car from us either by paying the full amount or by getting financing from our regular partners.

Your current car can also be used as part of the payment for a new one, but only after our physical inspection. After purchasing a car from us, you can service it with our regular partners. We also accept cars for consignment sale at our lot.

We value long-term cooperation and a transparent and trustworthy approach.

If you have any questions, please contact us — we will be happy to help.
